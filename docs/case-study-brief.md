# Case Study Brief: Smart Campus Timetable System

## Case Context
Students and lecturers frequently deal with timetable confusion on campus: classes get moved to different rooms, lecture times change at short notice, and exam or make-up class schedules are announced late. Right now, most of this information travels through scattered channels — printed timetables, WhatsApp/Telegram groups, email blasts, or notice boards — which are easy to miss, hard to search, and often out of date. Students end up unsure which room to go to, double-booking their own study time, or missing class changes entirely. Lecturers and academic admin staff also lack a simple, central way to publish and update schedule changes so that everyone sees the same accurate version at the same time.

## Target Users
- Student who needs to view, search, and stay updated on their personal class timetable
- Lecturer/staff who needs to publish or update class timings, rooms, or cancellations
- Academic/admin staff who manages the master timetable, room allocations, and conflict resolution across departments

## Product Direction
The proposed product is a web-based smart timetable system that allows students to view a personalized, always up-to-date class schedule, receive real-time notifications when a class time or room changes, and search for room/class availability. Lecturers can submit schedule changes or cancellations through the same platform, and admin staff can manage the master timetable, detect and resolve scheduling conflicts, and approve/publish updates so every user sees a single, reliable source of truth.
